I am a Ph.D. student at ROC-HCI group, University of Rochester, NY supervised by Prof. Ehsan Hoque.

Prior to this I have been a full-time Research Assistant at Department of CSE, BUET for nearly 3 years with Prof. Rifat Shahriyar and Prof. Anindya Iqbal. My work surrounded making AI's that understand human and programming language, and can change one into another.

Projects

  • I created Text2App, a framework that can turn a natural language text into functional Android applications.
  • I created an introductory Machine Learning course in my native language Bengali that dives into theory, practice, and intuition behind neural networks. (Email me for free access)
